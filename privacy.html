1. Who we are

iSATPrep ("we," "us," "our") provides online Digital SAT Reading & Writing preparation courses and practice tools at isatprep.net. This policy applies to that website and the services offered through it.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (or your Google account profile if you sign in with Google).
  • Age confirmation — your date of birth or confirmation that you are 13 or older, collected at signup.
  • Learning data — your course progress, practice answers, and saved positions, so you can pick up where you left off.
  • Communications — messages you send us (e.g., support emails).

Information collected automatically

  • Usage & device data — pages visited, features used, browser and device type, and approximate location (from IP address), collected via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — see Cookies & analytics.

Payment information (when you purchase)

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store your full card number — Stripe handles that directly. We receive only payment status and limited metadata (e.g., which plan you bought).

3. How we use information

  • To create and manage your account and sign you in.
  • To deliver the courses, save your progress, and unlock content you've purchased.
  • To process payments and provide receipts.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To understand how the service is used and improve it (analytics).
  • To send you service and, with your consent where required, occasional product emails. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • To protect the service against fraud and abuse, and to comply with law.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use student data for targeted advertising.

5. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential functions (like keeping you signed in) and for analytics via Google Analytics. Where required, we ask for your consent before loading non-essential analytics, and we honor browser Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Children & students under 18

iSATPrep is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

If you are between 13 and 17, please use iSATPrep with the involvement of a parent or guardian. We limit our collection of student information to what is needed to provide the service, and we do not sell student data or use it for targeted advertising.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live (for example, California and several other U.S. states), you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you and request a copy.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and personal information.
  • Opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell or share for cross-context behavioral advertising).
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@isatprep.net. You can also request account deletion from within your account settings. We will verify your request and respond within the timeframe required by law.

8. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal information, except where we must retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

9. How we protect your data

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest, access controls, and the security infrastructure of Google Cloud. Your data is stored in the United States. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and will notify affected users and regulators of a breach as required by law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you by email or through the service.
